Malindi Beach

Kilifi County Indian Ocean Kenya

Malindi is one of Kenya's oldest coastal towns, where Vasco da Gama erected a coral pillar in 1498 that still stands today. The town blends Swahili, Portuguese, and Italian influences—a significant Italian expat community gives it a unique Mediterranean-African flavour. Malindi Marine National Park, established in 1968, was Kenya's first marine protected area.

Key Information

Water Temperature
25-28°C year-round

July-March (avoid April-June rains)

Malindi Airport (MYD) with domestic flights; 120 km from Mombasa
