Lac Rose (Lake Retba)

Dakar Region Atlantic Coast Senegal

Lac Rose—officially Lake Retba—is one of Africa's most surreal landscapes. The shallow salt lake turns vivid pink due to Dunaliella salina algae, especially during the dry season (November-June). Salinity reaches 40%, higher than the Dead Sea, allowing effortless floating. Salt harvesters work waist-deep, their skin protected by shea butter. The lake lies just 1 km from the Atlantic, separated by coastal dunes. It was the original finish line of the Paris-Dakar Rally.
