Kribi Beach

South Region Atlantic Ocean Cameroon

Kribi is Cameroon's answer to the beach resort—a former fishing village transformed into a weekend escape for Douala and Yaoundé residents. The main attraction is the Chutes de la Lobé, one of the world's few places where a waterfall plunges directly into the sea. The surrounding rainforest is home to Baka (Pygmy) communities who offer guided forest walks. Fresh seafood, relaxed beach bars, and the unique waterfall create an experience unlike anywhere else in Central Africa.
