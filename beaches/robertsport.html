Robertsport Beach

Grand Cape Mount County Atlantic Ocean Liberia

Robertsport sits on a dramatic peninsula where Lake Piso meets the Atlantic Ocean. The town has emerged as West Africa's premier surf destination since civil war ended in 2003. Cassava Point and Cotton Trees offer world-class right-hand point breaks, while beach breaks suit beginners. The Liberian Surf Union has developed a community-based tourism model. The faded colonial architecture, fishing canoes, and slow pace recall an earlier era. Few tourists venture here, but those who do find consistent waves and genuine local warmth.
