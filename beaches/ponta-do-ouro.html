Ponta do Ouro

Maputo Province Indian Ocean Mozambique

Ponta do Ouro sits at Mozambique's southern tip, just north of the South African border. The area is renowned for its resident pod of bottlenose dolphins and exceptional scuba diving. The Ponta do Ouro Partial Marine Reserve protects 678 km² of ocean, including pristine coral reefs and important turtle nesting beaches. The village has evolved from a fishing settlement into an adventure sports hub.
