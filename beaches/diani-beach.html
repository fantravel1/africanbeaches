Diani Beach

Kenya South Coast, Kwale County Indian Ocean White Coral Sand

Diani Beach is a 17 km stretch of white sand on Kenya's south coast, about 30 km south of Mombasa. Protected by a coral reef that creates a calm lagoon, it has been repeatedly recognized at the World Travel Awards as Africa's leading beach destination. The area supports diverse marine life, and colobus monkeys inhabit the coastal forest behind the beach.

Access

Fly into Moi International Airport (MBA) in Mombasa or Ukunda Airstrip (UKA) near Diani. From Mombasa, cross the Likoni Ferry and drive south approximately 30 minutes. Matatus (shared minibuses) also connect Mombasa to the Diani area.
