Príncipe Beach

Príncipe Island Gulf of Guinea São Tomé and Príncipe

Príncipe is the smaller and even more remote of the two main islands, with just 8,000 inhabitants. The entire island is a UNESCO Biosphere Reserve, protecting one of Africa's most intact ecosystems. Praia Banana, consistently ranked among the world's most beautiful beaches, curves between jungle-covered headlands. The island was also where, in 1919, Arthur Eddington's eclipse observations confirmed Einstein's theory of general relativity—making it both naturally and scientifically significant.
