Saint-Gilles Beach

Saint-Paul Indian Ocean Réunion (France)

Saint-Gilles-les-Bains is Réunion's principal beach resort, located on the only part of this volcanic island with a coral-protected lagoon. The beaches at L'Ermitage and Boucan Canot offer calm turquoise waters ideal for families and snorkellers. Behind the coast, the dramatic Piton de la Fournaise volcano rises over 2,600 metres. As a French overseas department, Réunion combines tropical Indian Ocean beauty with European infrastructure, Creole culture, and exceptional cuisine blending French, African, Indian, and Chinese influences.
