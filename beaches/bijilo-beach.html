Bijilo Beach

Western Region Atlantic Ocean The Gambia

Bijilo sits at the southern end of The Gambia's tourism development area, where the Bijilo Forest Park meets the Atlantic. The 51-hectare park protects one of the last fragments of coastal woodland, home to green vervet monkeys and western red colobus. The beach stretches north toward Kololi, offering long walks on wide sand. The combination of wildlife and beach makes Bijilo unique among The Gambia's coastal resorts.
