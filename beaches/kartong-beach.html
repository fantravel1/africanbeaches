Kartong Beach

Western Region Atlantic Ocean The Gambia

Kartong is The Gambia's southernmost village, a fishing community where the Allahein River meets the Atlantic. Far from the northern resort strip, Kartong offers an authentic glimpse of coastal Gambian life. The Kartong Bird Observatory monitors migratory species, while the Folonko crocodile pool holds sacred significance for local communities. The beach is wild and undeveloped, with strong currents requiring caution for swimmers.
