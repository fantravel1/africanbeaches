Oran Beaches

Oran Province Mediterranean Sea Algeria

Oran is Algeria's second-largest city and the birthplace of raï music—a rebellious blend of Bedouin folk, Spanish flamenco, and modern pop. The coastline stretches 120 km with sandy beaches at Ain Turk, Les Andalouses, and Kristel. The city itself rises dramatically above the sea, with the Santa Cruz fortress and Ottoman-era old town overlooking the harbour. Oran's beaches are popular with Algerians but see few international tourists, offering authentic Mediterranean atmosphere at a fraction of European prices.
