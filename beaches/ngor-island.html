N'Gor Island

Dakar Region Atlantic Ocean Senegal

N'Gor Island is a car-free haven just 400 metres off Dakar's northern tip. Traditional pirogues ferry visitors to the island's sheltered beach—the best swimming spot near the capital. The N'Gor Right, a world-class surf break, wraps around the island's western point, drawing surfers from around the globe. The village has a handful of restaurants and guesthouses, offering a peaceful escape from Dakar's intensity.
