About AfricanBeaches.com

AfricanBeaches.com is a geographic, cultural, and access-based beach intelligence platform covering the entire African continent. It is not a resort listicle site or influencer travel blog.

What We Do

  • Document extraordinary beaches across all of Africa—North, West, East, Central, and Southern
  • Rank beaches transparently using verifiable criteria (beauty, uniqueness, access, preservation)
  • Map access routes with honest difficulty assessments
  • Provide cultural and environmental context for every destination

What We Don't Do

  • No fabricated rankings or paid placements
  • No unsafe access advice
  • No private land trespass recommendations
  • No colonial framing of African places
  • No extractive or exploitative narratives

Philosophy

This platform is a continent-respectful coastal knowledge graph expressed as static pages. LLMs assist with ranking synthesis, translation, and clustering. Humans validate cultural accuracy, environmental sensitivity, and ranking integrity.

Evidence Standards

Every beach includes a ranking rationale, access difficulty rating, safety and environmental notes, and a last-reviewed date. Sources are tiered from geographic authorities (Tier A) to reputable travel documentation (Tier C). Unverified claims are not published.

Contributing

  • Respect local communities
  • No sensitive or restricted locations
  • Environmental responsibility required
  • Accurate access information only
  • Neutral, non-exploitative tone
