Assinie

Sud-Comoé Region Atlantic Ocean Ivory Coast

Assinie is the beach destination of choice for Abidjan's elite—a long peninsula where the Aby Lagoon system meets the Gulf of Guinea. The area divides into Assinie-Mafia (the quieter lagoon side) and Assinie-France (the ocean beach). Luxury villas, beach hotels, and seafood restaurants line the coast. The combination of calm lagoon waters and Atlantic surf offers options for all preferences. It's Ivory Coast's closest equivalent to a Caribbean resort strip.
