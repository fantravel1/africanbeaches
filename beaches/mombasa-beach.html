Mombasa Beach

Mombasa County Indian Ocean Kenya

Mombasa, Kenya's second-largest city and principal port, offers a string of beaches along its northern and southern shores. The city's rich Swahili heritage, Portuguese fortifications, and Arab trading history provide cultural depth unmatched by purpose-built resorts. Nyali Beach to the north and the beaches of the South Coast offer warm, turquoise waters year-round.
