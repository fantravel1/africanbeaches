Beau Vallon

Seychelles Mahé Island Indian Ocean White Sand

Beau Vallon is the most popular beach on Mahé, Seychelles' main island. This 2 km crescent on the northwest coast offers calm, shallow waters ideal for swimming and water sports. Unlike the more remote Seychelles beaches, Beau Vallon has a lively atmosphere with beachside vendors, restaurants, and a weekly bazaar. It's the most accessible beach from the capital Victoria and the international airport.

Access

From Seychelles International Airport (SEZ), Beau Vallon is approximately 20 minutes by taxi. Public buses connect Victoria to Beau Vallon regularly. Most hotels on Mahé are within easy reach of this beach.
