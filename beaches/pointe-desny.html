Pointe d'Esny

Grand Port District Indian Ocean Mauritius

Pointe d'Esny is a residential peninsula on Mauritius' southeast coast, offering a quieter alternative to the island's tourist beaches. The shallow, sheltered lagoon is perfect for swimming, kayaking, and kitesurfing. The nearby Île aux Aigrettes, managed by the Mauritian Wildlife Foundation, is a restored nature reserve with endangered species including the pink pigeon and Telfair's skink.
