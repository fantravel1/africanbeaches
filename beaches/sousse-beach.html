Sousse Beach

Sousse Governorate Mediterranean Sea Tunisia

Sousse is Tunisia's third-largest city and the capital of the Sahel region. The UNESCO-listed medina features a 9th-century ribat (fortress-monastery) and atmospheric souks. The resort zone extends north through Port El Kantaoui, offering kilometres of sandy Mediterranean beaches. The combination of historic old town, lively nightlife, and beach resort facilities makes Sousse Tunisia's most popular package destination.
