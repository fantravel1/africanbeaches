Kololi Beach

The Gambia Senegambia Area Atlantic Ocean Golden Sand

Kololi Beach lies at the heart of The Gambia's tourist zone, the "Senegambia strip," offering easy access to West African beach culture. The beach stretches for several kilometres with golden sand, warm Atlantic waters, and a lively atmosphere of beach bars, craft vendors, and local life. It's the most accessible introduction to beach tourism in this part of Africa, with good infrastructure and a welcoming local community.

Access

Banjul International Airport (BJL) is 15 km away with direct flights from Europe. The Senegambia area has numerous hotels, restaurants, and tourist facilities. Taxis are affordable and plentiful. The beach is easily walked from most Kololi accommodation.

Local Culture

The Gambia is known as "The Smiling Coast" for its friendly people. Beach vendors sell crafts, offer tours, and become familiar faces. The Senegambia strip has West Africa's most developed tourist nightlife. For a more local experience, visit the nearby fish market at Bakau or take a pirogue trip up the Gambia River.
