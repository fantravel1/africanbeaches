Stone Town Beach

Zanzibar City Indian Ocean Tanzania

Stone Town's waterfront is where Africa, Arabia, India, and Europe have met for over a millennium. The UNESCO World Heritage Site features the famous Forodhani Gardens night food market, the old dhow harbour, and the House of Wonders. While not a swimming beach, the waterfront offers spectacular sunsets and serves as the gateway to Zanzibar's archipelago.

Key Information

Best Time to Visit
Sunset for Forodhani market
Best Months
June-October, December-February
Getting There
Abeid Amani Karume Airport (ZNZ), 8 km from town
