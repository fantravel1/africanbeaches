Ifaty Beach

Atsimo-Andrefana Region Mozambique Channel Madagascar

Ifaty is a collection of Vezo fishing villages north of Toliara (Tuléar) on Madagascar's southwest coast. The area offers the best combination of beach access and unique Malagasy ecosystems—the nearby spiny forest contains bizarre baobabs and octopus trees found nowhere else on Earth. The lagoon is protected by a coral reef, and traditional outrigger pirogues dot the shoreline. Whale watching (July-September) adds seasonal interest.
