Flic en Flac

Mauritius West Coast Indian Ocean White Sand

Flic en Flac is a lively west coast beach known for spectacular sunsets, a long stretch of white sand, and easy access to the Black River Gorges National Park. The lagoon is reef-protected with calm, clear water ideal for swimming and snorkeling. More developed than the east coast, Flic en Flac has restaurants, shops, and a vibrant local scene mixing tourists and Mauritians.

Access

SSR International Airport is approximately 45 minutes away via the motorway. The beach stretches for several kilometres with public access points, free parking, and basic facilities. Many visitors combine beach time with trips to Black River Gorges (15 minutes by car) or Casela Nature Park.
