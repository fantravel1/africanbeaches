Blue Bay

Grand Port District Indian Ocean Mauritius

Blue Bay is Mauritius' premier snorkelling destination, protected as a marine park since 2000. The shallow lagoon contains over 50 species of coral and countless tropical fish. The water's extraordinary blue colour comes from the white sand bottom and the lagoon's depth. Glass-bottom boat trips reveal the underwater gardens to non-swimmers, while the crescent beach provides excellent swimming.
