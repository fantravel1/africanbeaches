Sal Rei

Boa Vista Island Atlantic Ocean Cape Verde

Sal Rei is the main town of Boa Vista, the easternmost and most desert-like of Cape Verde's inhabited islands. The town beach offers calm turquoise waters, while the island's endless beaches (Praia de Chaves, Praia de Santa Mónica) attract kitesurfers and those seeking solitude. Boa Vista hosts the world's third-largest loggerhead turtle nesting population, with conservation projects offering night-time turtle watching from June to October.
