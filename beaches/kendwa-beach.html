Kendwa Beach

Tanzania Zanzibar (Northwest Coast) Indian Ocean White Sand

Kendwa Beach sits on Zanzibar's northwest coast, just south of Nungwi. Unlike the east coast beaches, Kendwa is swimmable at all tides due to deeper water close to shore. It's known for its west-facing sunset views and monthly full moon parties that draw travellers from across the island. The beach maintains a social, backpacker-friendly atmosphere while offering upmarket options as well.

Coordinates
5.76°S, 39.23°E

Access

From Stone Town or the airport, Kendwa is approximately 1 hour by taxi. Dalla-dallas run to Nungwi, from where Kendwa is a short walk or tuk-tuk ride. Many visitors combine Kendwa and Nungwi stays.
